IB Mathematics SL

Internal Assessment Paper

This is a paper I wrote for my Math class's internal assessment. It's a mathematical analysis of the most fuel efficient orbital altitude for a satellite. Fuel in a rocket launch, especially one for a satellite, goes to two purposes: To get the satellite into space and to counter the amount of air resistance at that altitude. This paper explores the relationship between the two and finds out where the fuel takeup is the least. A full PDF of the paper is given here!
